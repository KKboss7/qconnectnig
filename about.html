Who We Are

Utilising innovation, technology, networks, and a wide range of industry experience, Qconnectniglimited. is a multi-faceted, forward-thinking oil and gas company that offers advisory, consultancy, trading, and capacity building services to clients throughout the oil and gas value chain (upstream, midstream, and downstream) in Africa.

Our company, which was incorporated in 2019 and is based in Nigeria, focuses on assets that are spread out across the African continent. A carefully selected group of technical professionals carry this out. As a business, we have invested a lot of time and money in research and development, or "R & D," which has allowed us to develop a line of goods that is truly unique in the market thanks to our in-house, technically astute upstream professionals who have a combined 55 years of expertise.

Qconnect has also analysed and implemented new corporate public-private partnership concepts and policies as we actively pursue capacity expansion through regulatory compliance.
